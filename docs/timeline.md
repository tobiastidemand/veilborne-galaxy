# The Veilborn — Master Chronology

> **The single source of truth for dates.** Whenever a date is written anywhere else
> (`lore.md` Canon VI, faction entries, system entries), it must agree with this file.
> If a new event needs placing, add it here first, then reference it.

## How dating works
- All dates are **relative to the present** — **"YA" = years before the in-fiction
  "now."** There is **no fixed in-world calendar** (a DM sets their own "now").
- Only two numbers are **hard canon**: the **Second Theft = 222 YA** and the
  **Quickening = 69 years long.** Everything older is **approximate** and may be
  nudged, never contradicted.
- 🔓 **PUBLIC** = common knowledge in-galaxy. 🔒 **HIDDEN** = Foundational Canon
  (GM-only); the public has a cover story or nothing.

## Consistency rules (so the timeline can't break)
1. **222 YA and the 69-yr Quickening are fixed.** Everything else flexes around them.
2. **The Stargiver must be mortal from his birth until 222 YA** — his pre-immortal
   span (birth → 222 YA) must fit a Keth'aal lifespan (**200–250 yrs**). Born **~430
   YA**, he is **~208** at the Second Theft — inside the lifespan, near its natural end.
   Don't push his birth earlier than ~472 YA (that would make him >250 at the theft).
3. **The Devourer is as old as the First Reckoning (~3,000–4,000 YA)** — not older,
   not "billions of years." Its Chorus's oldest voices are the Keth'aal it ate then.
4. **The Coming (~330 YA) must precede the Quickening, which must precede the Charting,
   which must precede the Second Theft (222 YA).** Order is canon; the gaps are soft.
5. **Any new dated event gets added here first**, then mirrored into `lore.md`.
